St. George and the dragon is a classic archetypal story about a knight who saves the maiden from a beast. But who exactly was George?

  • Greek
  • Roman Soldier, member of Praetorian Guard for emperor Diocletian
  • Martyred for refusing to recant
  • His memorial, Saint George's Day, is traditionally celebrated on 23 April
